Website Planning Document

Site Name

SafeTrack HSE Portal

The name “SafeTrack” reflects the goal of tracking and improving workplace safety. It is designed for Health, Safety, and Environmental management in industrial environments, especially within pharmaceutical and manufacturing organizations.

Site Purpose

SafeTrack is a web-based platform that allows organizations to report, monitor, and manage workplace health, safety, and environmental incidents. It supports incident reporting, corrective actions tracking, safety audits, and compliance monitoring across multiple facilities.

Scenarios

  • How can an employee quickly report a workplace injury or hazard?
  • How can safety managers track recurring incidents across different sites?

Color Scheme

  • Deep Green (#1f6f54): Used for headers, branding, and trust indicators.
  • Orange (#f39c12): Used for alerts, warnings, and highlights.
  • Light Gray (#f4f6f8): Used for page background.
  • Dark Text (#2c2c2c): Used for readability in paragraphs.

Typography

  • Inter: Used for all headings and body text for a clean, professional UI.
